About Us

Arzac Engineering (M) Sdn Bhd (AESB) is a 100% Bumiputera-owned company established in 2020, focused on delivering reliable engineering and ICT solutions for today’s fast-moving world.

From ICT systems and telecommunications to civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering, we provide end-to-end services designed to support business growth and operational efficiency. Our capabilities also extend to ICT trading, building material supply, and facility-related services.

Backed by a dedicated and forward-thinking team, we aim to grow alongside Malaysia’s development—creating value, opportunities, and solutions that make a difference.

Building solutions. Empowering progress.
